Busch Gardens Williamsburg - Best Route

About this map

Busch Gardens Williamsburg has a large collection of experiences, shows, and attractions including 11 world-class roller coasters.

In order to experience every roller coaster in the park, you'll turn left after the park entrance. You will visit England, Sesame Street Forest of Fun, Festa Italia, Oktoberfest, Germany, New France, France, Ireland, and Scotland.
